\frametitle{Conclusion}

\small
\textbf{What I showed:}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Time protection mechanisms \textit{can} be integrated into an existing mainstream OS
  \item Domain-level scheduler achieves constant-time scheduling in QEMU
  \item Trade-offs in complexity, performance, and usability are significant
\end{itemize}

\vspace{0.15cm}
\textbf{Future work:}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Evaluation on real hardware with \texttt{fence.t} support (or gem5)
  \item Safeguarding system calls: critical sections delay scheduling
  \item Remaining channels: interrupts, higher-level caches
\end{itemize}

\vspace{0.15cm}
\textbf{Takeaway:} Selective temporal partitioning may be more practical than
full domain isolation for general-purpose systems.

\end{frame}
